About Me

Hello! My Name is Gilberto Rascon and I am 26 years old. I was born and raised in Tucson, AZ. My parents are from Mexico and I speak both English and Spanish. I graduated from the University of Arizona in 2014 with a B.A in Anthropology and have been working at non-profits for the last 3 years. I have mostly worked with children and immigrant populations from Central America.

My career goal at the moment is to fully transition into the field of web development after completing the University of Arizona Coding Bootcamp and to become a front-end web developer. I am eager to develop the necessary skills to be able to create and maintain efficient and functional web pages that have awesome designs in addition to optimal functioning. I value working as a team as well as being able to create something new that ultimately makes life easier for those using it.
